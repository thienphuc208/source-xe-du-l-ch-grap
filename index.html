About Cai Lậy District

Cai Lậy is a district-level town in Tiền Giang Province, Vietnam. Located in the Mekong Delta region, it serves as an important transportation hub connecting various provinces. Our taxi services cover all areas within Cai Lậy district and beyond, providing reliable transportation for both locals and visitors.
